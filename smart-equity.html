Equity Token

Smart Equity

A digital asset that combines all the value of multiple service providers into that digital share.

What is it?

Smart Equity means building the features and functions of traditional services including exchanges, share registries, corporate secretarial, investor relations and administration into each digital share to deliver those layers of value cheaply, easily and efficiently, and in-so-doing create tremendous opportunities for shareholder value and innovation.
