Welcome to the Community Hub

At Enhanced Society we aim to live up to our name and provide a platform that allows the community to collaborate and contribute whilst being rewarded for their efforts. You'll find 'quality' is at the core of everything we focus on and as a result develop tools that focus on truth and validation.

Our Learning Framework

Filter

We need to learn how to filter through this crazy world of information overload, social media mesmerisation and disconnection with our human race to obtain clarity on what is most integral to our existence.

Experiement

To test our learning we experiment. We challenge the status quo. We must take risks to ensure we are continually learning so we can make a more positive contribution within our community.

Teach

We teach what we know in order to share our knowledge and confirm our own understanding of the information we have learnt. Therefore we are learning to teach and consolidation of what’s real and what’s made up can be processed.
