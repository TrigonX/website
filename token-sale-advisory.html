Phase 2 - Private Token Sale

Execute communication strategies, develop a community of early investors and commence smart contract development and auditing.

Legal analysis of token and security implementation, execute on community management plan and deploy full scale marketing strategy.

Timing: 1 - 2 months

Phase 4 - Public Token Sale

Management of marketing channels, partnerships and promotions, whilst providing high level engagement to the growing community.

Timing: 4 - 6 weeks

Phase 5 - Post Token Offering

Distribution of tokens, ongoing governance, escrow and exchange management.

Timing: 4 - 6 weeks
